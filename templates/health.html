Daily Health Tips

Expert recommendations for a healthier lifestyle

Nutrition Guidelines

  • Eat a rainbow of fruits and vegetables daily
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains
  • Stay hydrated with 8 glasses of water daily

Physical Activity

  • 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily
  • Mix cardio with strength training
  • Take regular walking breaks during work

Mental Wellness

  • Practice daily meditation or mindfulness
  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule
  • Take breaks from digital devices

Daily Health Practices

Morning Routine

  • Morning Exercise

Evening Routine

  • Digital Sunset

    Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime

  • Sleep Schedule

    Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep
